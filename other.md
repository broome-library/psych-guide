# Other Resources

## [**Classics in the History of Psychology**](https://web.archive.org/web/20150906083433/http:/psychclassics.yorku.ca/)

The CHP website explores classics and seminal works that have influenced the history of psychologydating from ancient times to present day. The CHP is indexed both by [author](https://web.archive.org/web/20150906083433/http:/psychclassics.yorku.ca/author.htm) and by [topic](https://web.archive.org/web/20150906083433/http:/psychclassics.yorku.ca/topic.htm), as well as suggested primary source readings.
