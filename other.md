### [**Classics in the History of Psychology**](http:/psychclassics.yorku.ca/)

The CHP website explores classics and seminal works that have influenced the history of psychologydating from ancient times to present day. The CHP is indexed both by [author](http:/psychclassics.yorku.ca/author.htm) and by [topic](http:/psychclassics.yorku.ca/topic.htm), as well as suggested primary source readings.
